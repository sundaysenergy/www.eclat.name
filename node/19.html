What he does…


Éclat entertains at events,
writing caricatures for party guests.

Éclat draws personalities...with words:


One smiling eye
skimming the surface
like a periscope.

The other,
frantic,
seaching the ocean floor
for scraps.

You
are the point
of convergence,
as I try to build
a boat that floats.

     M.L.'s Graduation Party
     San Rafael, California © March 10, 2002
