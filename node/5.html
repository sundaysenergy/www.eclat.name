What he does…


Éclat performs a unique form of entertainment.   


Éclat draws personalities...with words.

Éclat looks into your eyes and creates
an original written portrayal of you.

Some call it caricature.
Some call it literature.
Some call it magic.
Some call it art. 

You and your guests enter a world
of insight, wit and beauty:
the realm of Éclat!
